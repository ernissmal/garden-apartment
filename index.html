About Our Garden Apartment

Offering garden and inner courtyard views, our Garden Apartment is located in Cēsis, just 1.4 km from INSIGNIA Art Gallery and 1.5 km from Sculpture Battle with Centaurus. This property offers access to a patio, free private parking, and free WiFi. Castle Park is 1.7 km away and St. John's Church is 1.5 km from the holiday home.

Brand new apartment with access to a beautiful garden. Renovated and operating since 2019, our property provides the perfect blend of modern comfort and natural tranquility.
